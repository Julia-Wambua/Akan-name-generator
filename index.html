AKAN NAMES

Akan names from Ghana are deeply meaningful, often determined by the day of the week a child is born, their birth order, or specific circumstances. These traditional names represent identity and legacy, with day names for both males and females, e.g., Kwasi/Akosua for Sunday, Kwame/Ama for Saturday.
